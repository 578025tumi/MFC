Enquiries & Auction Guide

Strict Registration Process:
  1. Download form or visit MFC branch.
  2. Provide FICA documents (ID, Proof of Residence).
  3. Pay refundable R5,000 deposit.
  4. Receive Buyers Code. Without this code, security will deny entry.

Select vehicles are available for immediate purchase ("Buy Now"). You must contact the Finance Manager to view the "Buy Now" catalogue. Prices are fixed in ZAR and include VAT.

Auctions are held weekly. Viewing is by appointment only. Bidding can be done on the floor (with Buyers Code) or online via the MFC portal. The highest bid above reserve wins.

Can I finance a car? Yes, Nedbank consultants are on-site.
Are cars tested? Yes, DEKRA reports are available for all vehicles.
What if I don't buy? Your deposit is refunded within 48 hours.
